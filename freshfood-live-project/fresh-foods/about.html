About Us

Fresh Food was created from a desire for a cozy, clean and hip neighborhood juice and more. We invite you to our store to snack on healthy eats, sip a spectacularly healthy drink and shop for healthy goods.
At Fresh Food we believe in local, humane, organic, etc. not to be trendy, but because we believe that is possible for a sustainable food system to exist.We love meeting the people who walk inour doors everyday. We are so thankful for each and every person who has helped make Fresh Food.
